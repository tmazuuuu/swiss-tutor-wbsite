How it works

A system designed to make you pass

SwissTutor is not just tutoring. It is a structured academic programme with built-in accountability at every step.

1

Set your target

Define your goal grade before starting. This is your benchmark.

2

Get matched

We assign you a peer tutor who successfully passed the exam in your exact course - with the same syllabus and exam style.

3

Weekly sessions

1 or 2 tutoring sessions per week, structured around your weak points.

4

Weekly preparation assignments

Receive preparation assignments every week, tailored to your course syllabus.

5

Adaptive intensity

If your results fall below your target trajectory, we increase the workload. You must accept this to keep the money-back guarantee.

✓

If you do the work, you pass. If you do the work and still score below 4.0 at HSG, we refund CHF 100.

Our guarantee

We put our money where our mouth is

If you complete all your assignments, attend all your sessions, and still score below 4.0 at HSG, we refund CHF 100. No questions asked.

You keep the guarantee if:

  • You submit 100% of weekly assignments on time
  • You attend all tutoring sessions (or cancel 24h in advance)

You lose the guarantee if:

  • You miss more than 1 assignment per month
  • You skip a session without 24h notice more than once

The guarantee exists for students who work hard. Not as a shortcut.
